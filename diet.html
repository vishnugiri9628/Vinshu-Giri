Nutrition Blog Post Ideas

To make things easier, we’ve done all of the keyword research for you. Here’s a comprehensive list of the healthy eating topics that people are searching for online. It’s the perfect source of creative nutrition blog post ideas…

1. Healthy food – the nutritional properties of different foods, food groups, or diets.

2. Nutrition connections – the links between nutrition and health, food, exercise, fitness, wellness, and physical activity.

3. Healthy eating – tips, habits, and advice on how to make healthy eating easier.

4. Student-specific articles – nutrition articles for students or kids that cover healthy eating and the importance of a balanced diet for school students in particular.

5. Information on a balanced diet – types of dietary patterns, infographics, the food pyramid, and eatwell plate.

6. Nutrients – carbohydrate, protein, fats, vitamins, minerals, fibre, and calories.

7. Why it matters – the importance of a healthy or balanced diet combined with exercise.

8. Nutrition research – scientific information, scholarly reviews, the latest nutrition research findings.

9. Hot topics – trending nutrition topics, the latest news, and recent developments in the field.

10. Benefits & disadvantages – such as the benefits of eating meat, a vegan diet, fasting, and any number of other dietary topics.

11. Nutrition by life stage – teenage nutrition, nutrition and aging, early childhood nutrition, etc.

12. Weight management – dieting, food addiction, and strategies for increasing body weight.

13. Nutrigenomics – the relationship between genes, nutrition, and health, along with associated research.

14. Sports nutrition – beyond everyday fitness, this is about nutrition for athletes and bodybuilders.

15. Guilty pleasures – fast food and junk food nutrition.

16. Nutrition for organisations – examining nutrition in schools or the workplace.

17. Commonly misunderstood topics – such as dietary supplements, food labels, and holistic nutrition.

18. Events – awareness days, nutrition month, or related current events.

19. Eating & mental health – emotional eating, triggers, stress, coping strategies, body image, eating disorders.

20. Separating fact from fiction – articles exposing nutrition quackery, false health claims, and bad nutrition.
